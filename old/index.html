In more than 7 words

Hi, I'm Elliot! I'm an architect turned building scientist turned information scientist. I guess that makes me part designer, part scientist, and part engineer. I made the move from architect to building scientist from a desire to make the built environment a more sustainable place. My interest in information visualization and experience developing educational software for the building industry then led me to the I School where I have found my calling in User Experience design.
