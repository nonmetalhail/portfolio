Hi, I’m Elliot! I’m an architect turned building scientist turned information scientist. I pride myself on being a generalist, part designer, part scientist, and part engineer and well versed in both atoms and bits.

With a Masters degree from the School of Information at UC Berkeley (named one of the world’s best design schools by Bussiness Inisder) I am well versed adherent of the Human Centered Design process.

In school and my professional career, I have worked on both physical and digital products including web, mobile, wearables, automotive infotainment head units, and other consumer products. I can lend expertise in user experience, human-computer interaction, product design, prototyping, and have worked in the audio domain, automotive domain, education, and building sector with expertise in construction and systems such as HVAC, lighting, envelope, etc.

Outside work, I enjoy the hiking-photography nexus, cooking delicious meals, baking bread and fancy cakes, nerding out over TED talks, and playing my guitar or listening to music (from bluegrass to the symphony).
